2026 Atlantic AI Summit
Sponsorship Package

📅 June 3-5, 2026 | 📍 University of New Brunswick, Fredericton

The 2026 Atlantic AI Summit is Atlantic Canada's leading platform for collaboration across industry, academia, and government, bringing together decision makers, innovators, and emerging talent to explore how artificial intelligence is being applied in real world contexts. Over three days, the Summit connects policy, research, and industry deployment through meaningful dialogue, strategic partnerships, and practical insight across sectors shaping the region's future.

What to Expect

🎤 3 Days of Executive and Applied AI Sessions
🌍 400 participants from across academia, industry, government and the innovation ecosystem
🏛 40+ Academic & Industry Partners
🎙 Sector leading speakers and panelists

Strategic Cross Sector Themes

  • AI Ethics, Trust & Governance
  • Responsible AI Deployment & Adoption
  • AI for Society, Arts & Humanities
  • AI & Climate Impact

Sector & Industry Applications

Applied use cases demonstrating how AI is transforming key industries and economic sectors, including:

  • AI in Healthcare
  • AI in Ocean & Environmental Technologies
  • AI in Cybersecurity
  • AI in Entertainment
  • AI in Defense
  • AI for Business & Industrial Innovation

Talent & Future Workforce

Focused on developing talent pathways, industry readiness, and future workforce capacity across Atlantic Canada.

  • AI for the Next Generation (Youth in AI)

Why Sponsor the 2026 Atlantic AI Summit?

The Atlantic AI Summit brings together decision-makers, researchers, practitioners, policymakers, and emerging talent from across Atlantic Canada and beyond. Sponsoring the Summit positions your organization at the centre of the region's AI ecosystem, connecting you directly with the people shaping how AI is developed, governed, and applied in real-world contexts.

Strategic Brand Alignment

Align your organization with innovation, applied research, and responsible AI adoption. Sponsoring the Summit signals a commitment to forward-looking technology, ethical deployment, and collaboration across academia, industry, and government.

Exclusive Networking & Engagement

Gain direct, on-site access to AI researchers, industry leaders, policymakers, and emerging talent through curated networking moments, including the Summit mixer. Sponsors are invited, visibly acknowledged, and supported with banner presence in key gathering areas to enable meaningful connections.

Supporting Talent & Regional Capacity

Sponsorship directly enables student participation, applied research showcases, and skills-development initiatives across the Summit program. By supporting the event, sponsors help lower barriers for students and early-career participants while strengthening the region's AI talent pipeline.

A Regional Platform with Real Impact

The Summit is designed to move beyond discussion, creating space for practical insight, collaboration, and applied outcomes across key sectors including health, climate, energy, cybersecurity, oceans, agriculture, and business innovation.
